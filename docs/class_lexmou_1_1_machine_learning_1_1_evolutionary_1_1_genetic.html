§ SelectionOneElement()

Vector<float> Lexmou.MachineLearning.Evolutionary.Genetic.SelectionOneElement ( )

Fitness proportionate selection of one element.

If \( f_{i} \) is the fitness of individual \( i \) in the population, its probability of being selected is \( p_i = \frac{f_i}{\Sigma_{j=1}^{N} f_j} \), where \( N \) is the number of individuals in the population.

Returns
A MathNet.numerics.Vector of the selected individual
